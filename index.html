Retail Intelligence FAQ
Executive Clarifications for Growth-Focused Retailers

Clear answers for independent UK retailers exploring predictive shrinkage analytics, POS integration, and scalable SaaS deployment.

01. How is this different from traditional POS reporting?

+
Traditional POS systems show past sales data. Retail Intelligence acts as a predictive layer that analyses risk signals, SKU behaviour, delivery timing, and store patterns to forecast shrinkage before financial impact occurs.

02. Does the platform require hardware installation?

+
No hardware, no cameras, and no surveillance systems. It is a secure SaaS intelligence platform that integrates directly with your existing POS infrastructure.

03. How quickly can retailers see measurable ROI?

+
Most pilot stores identify shrinkage anomalies within the first 30–45 days. Early-stage detection significantly reduces stock loss and improves operational margin.

04. Is this suitable for single-store retailers?

+
Yes. The dashboard is designed for 1–10 store operations. The system prioritises clarity, simplicity, and actionable intelligence without requiring technical expertise.

05. How secure is the platform?

+
Enterprise-grade encrypted cloud hosting ensures compliance with UK data regulations. No customer surveillance or sensitive identity tracking is used.

06. Can it scale with multi-store expansion?

+
Yes. The architecture supports centralised multi-store dashboards, comparative risk analytics, and performance benchmarking across locations.

07. What makes this ideal for UK independent retailers?

+
The platform was designed specifically for UK SME retailers facing rising theft, margin pressure, and operational blind spots. It provides predictive control without enterprise-level cost.
